Tiny Swords scene milestone assets.

The repository did not contain the downloaded Tiny Swords Free Pack zip, so the grass, rock/cliff, water background, and foam PNGs were extracted or cropped from the public Sprite Fusion Tiny Swords editor page.

Original artist: Pixel Frog.
Sprite Fusion source page: https://www.spritefusion.com/editor/tiny-swords
Original Tiny Swords page: https://pixelfrog-assets.itch.io/tiny-swords

Kept files:
- water-background.png
- grass-tiles.png
- grass-center.png
- scene-layout.png
- water-foam.png

water-foam.png is cropped from the bottom water/foam row of scene-layout.png so the compact rock island can show foam directly under the rock.
